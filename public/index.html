MSCE Lab


MSCE ("Megascale") stands for Multiscale "Modeling Simulations, Computing and Experiments". At the MSCE lab, we are dedicated to tackle challenges related to water, energy, resources and infrastructure by using fundamental insights to solve practical problems related to . We integrate theories, modeling, simulations, and experiments to develop technologies for water treatment, resource recovery, and energy storage systems. Our ultimate goal is to facilitate the transition of these technologies from the laboratory to commercial and full-scale applications. We are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive research environment that welcomes highly motivated students from diverse backgrounds.
