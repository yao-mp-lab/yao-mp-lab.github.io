Yao Multiphysics Lab

At our lab, we are dedicated to using fundamental insights to solve practical problems related to water, energy, and infrastructure. We take a multiscale approach that integrates theories, modeling, simulations, and experiments to develop design and operation guidelines for technologies such as water treatment, resource recovery, and energy storage systems. Our ultimate goal is to facilitate the transition of these technologies from the laboratory to commercial and full-scale applications. We are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive research environment that welcomes highly motivated students from diverse backgrounds.
